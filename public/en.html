Kike Estrada

About

Kike Estrada is a singer, composer, guitarist, arranger, and music director, with more than 20 years of experience spanning secular and Christian music.

He has taken part in more than 100 recordings and music productions: as a guest across different roles, and as artistic producer, arranger, and composer on his own projects.

Throughout his career he has also worked as a director and mentor of musicians, supporting preparation and artistic development in both scenes.

Artist

With more than 21 years in the entertainment industry, I offer a versatile musical show —ballads, boleros, tropical, rock, and songs in English, Portuguese, and Jewish music— for weddings, private parties, and performances in theaters, hotels, bars, and casinos nationwide. Punctual, professional, and respectful, I am a reliable ally to make your event memorable from start to finish.
